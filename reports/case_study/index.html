Panel 03 · Pass@k convergence

Drag k to watch the gap to thinking close on AIME

Both models sampled n=16 per problem on the same 20 AIME 24+25 problems. Panel trails at every k in the measured range — but the gap narrows monotonically with k. Drag the slider; the gap value updates live.

k = 1

At k=1: panel pass@k = 22.5%, thinking pass@k = 73.1%, gap = −50.6pp. Closure from k=1 to here: 0pp.

Panel 04 · Compute asymmetry

How many RL steps fits inside your compute budget?

Panel rollouts terminate compactly inside the debate (~640 ac_tokens). Thinking rollouts run near max_tokens (~11,000 ac_tokens). The difference compounds: you can do roughly 12× more panel-arm RL steps than thinking-arm RL steps for the same wall-time on Tinker.
